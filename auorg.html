Abstract

This ontology defines a series of sub classes of the generic org:Organization class to describe types of Australian Government organisations represented in the website directory.data.gov.au. It also defines a series of properties for organisations to capture specific properties of those organisations also given in directory.gov.au.

This ontology is incomplete as of v0.5 and is currently for demo purposes only.
